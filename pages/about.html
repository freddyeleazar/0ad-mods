About 0 A.D. Mods

Welcome to the 0 A.D. Mod Repository

This project serves as a dedicated platform for the 0 A.D. game community to discover, download, and share custom modifications that enhance gameplay and expand the ancient civilization simulator's capabilities.

Our Mission

We aim to create a centralized, professional showcase for high-quality 0 A.D. mods, making it easy for players to find modifications that align with their gaming preferences while ensuring compatibility and ease of installation.

Featured Mods

Currently available are three premium mods:

  • Day/Night Cycle - A dynamic day/night cycle overlay with real-time atmospheric effects, adding visual immersion to each match without performance impact.
  • Personal Names - Replaces generic unit names with historically accurate personal names based on civilization and gender, featuring 13 ancient civilizations and over 500 unique names.
  • Experience Skills - An RPG-style progression system where units gain 14 distinct skills through gameplay, creating specialized warriors, gatherers, and builders.

Compatibility

All mods are designed for 0 A.D. 0.28.0+ and prioritize cross-platform support. They undergo rigorous testing to ensure they don't break existing gameplay mechanics.

Contribution Guidelines

We welcome mod submissions from the community. All mods must follow the standard archive structure and include proper documentation for installation and configuration.
